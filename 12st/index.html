BABA BANDA SINGH BAHADUR ENGINEERING COLLEGE

The college is approved by the A.I.C.T.E, New Delhi, Ministry of H.R.D, GOI and is affiliated to I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University, Jalandhar.
The college has earned third time accreditation from the NBA for various programmes. BBSBEC has also been accredited by the Institution
of Engineers (India). Presently, the college runs six B.Tech Programs, two B.Vocational courses, five M.Tech. Programs and MBA. It is one of
the leading institutions in Engineering & Technology and is the most sought-after for admission by the students.
